# Operator Precedence Summary

Operators are listed from highest to lowest precedence:

1. Primary expressions (literals, identifiers, function calls, parentheses)
2. Unary operators (`not`, `-`)
3. Multiplicative (`*`, `/`, `%`)
4. Additive (`+`, `-`)
5. Relational (`<`, `<=`, `>`, `>=`, `is`)
6. Equality (`==`, `!=`)
7. Logical AND (`and`)
8. Logical OR (`or`)
9. Ternary conditional (`?:`)
